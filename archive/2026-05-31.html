Workday Puts Its Sana Self-Service Agent Inside Microsoft 365 Copilot — the HR Front Door Just Moved Into the Collaboration Layer
HOT Workday Agentic
On 13 May Workday made its Sana Self-Service Agent generally available inside Microsoft 365 Copilot, so employees can ask HR and finance questions — leave balance, time-off requests, expense status, review tasks, policy look-ups — and have them actioned without leaving Microsoft 365. The design choice is the story: Copilot is the conversational surface, but the transaction still runs through Workday’s deterministic business processes, approvals and permissions, so the system of record stays authoritative. For Deloitte HRSD practitioners this is the clearest signal yet that the employee “front door” is migrating into the collaboration layer (Copilot, Teams, Slack) — exactly the surface ServiceNow EmployeeWorks and Otto also contest.
