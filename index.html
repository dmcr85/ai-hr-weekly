DeepSeek V4: 1 Trillion Parameters on Huawei Chips, MIT Licence — Expected Within Weeks
WATCH DeepSeek
As of 11 April 2026, DeepSeek V4 has not been publicly released, but Reuters reported on 3 April that it is expected "within the next few weeks."
